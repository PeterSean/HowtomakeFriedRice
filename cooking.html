HOW TO COOK FRIED RICE

COOKING

Step 1

Get the pan nice and hot, then add your sesame oil.

Step 2

Add you minced garlic and ginger, and mix well for 10 minutes. Make sure to season on every step.

Step 4

Add the rice, and mix around well. Get the rice covered with that garlic and ginger infused oil. keep mising for about ten minutes. Mix the rice gently so that you dont break the rice.

Step 5

Move the rice to one side, and add in your eggs and mix until the eggs are 75% cooked. Make sure season your food with salt and pepper.

Step 6

Add your soy sauce, keep adding until the rice is light brown in color.

Step 7

Add in the green Onions, and mix well. Make sure to season.

Step 8

You are done! You ready to eat and enjoy!
